Salary Increment Letter

Subject: Requesting to Increase My Salary Package


Respected Sir/Madam,


I am writing to formally request a revision of my current salary package. Along with my experience, my responsibilities have also increased significantly and I am now also covering additional duties. This, of course, requires more effort, skill, and time.

I have consistently performed my duties diligently and have contributed positively to the team. I request you to consider increasing my salary so that I can continue to work harder and you can expect even better results from me. I trust you will give my request your due consideration.


Thanking you,


Yours sincerely,


_(Your Name)
