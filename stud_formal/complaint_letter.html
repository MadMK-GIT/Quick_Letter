Complaint Letter about Food

The Principal,

Subject: Complaint Regarding Food Quality


Respected Sir/Madam,


I am writing to complain about a meal we had in our canteen yesterday. We had ordered food but had to wait for more than 20 minutes to get it. From a menu of 12 dishes, only a few were available and their quality was poor.

We have eaten in our canteen several times in the past but this is the first time we have received such bad treatment. I am not asking for a refund but I would like you to improve the quality of your dishes and service.


Thanking you,


Yours faithfully,


_(Your Name)
